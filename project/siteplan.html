Site Name

Elite Group of Companies

This name was selected because it directly represents the brand as a multi-sector enterprise committed to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

The purpose of this site is to provide a comprehensive understanding of Elite Group of Companies by outlining its operations, core values, and the role it plays in delivering high-quality services across multiple sectors.

Scenarios

  • “What services does Elite Group of Companies provide across different sectors?”
  • “Where can I learn more about the company’s recent projects and achievements?”

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Deep Blue (#004080) — used for headings and navigation.

Accent Color: Orange (#e67e22) — used for highlights, buttons, and call-to-action elements.

Background: Light Gray (#f4f4f9) — used for page background.

Typography

Headings: Georgia, serif — chosen for professionalism and readability.

Body Text: Arial, sans-serif — chosen for clarity and modern look.

Wireframe

Mobile View: Single-column layout with stacked sections (banner, mission, services, projects, contact).

Desktop/Tablet View: Multi-column layout with navigation bar at the top, banner image, and grid sections for services/projects.
